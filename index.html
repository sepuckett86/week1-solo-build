My Favorite Animals

Here are my favorite animals:

  • Sloth
  • Manatee
  • Panda

Sloth

This is my favorite animal. I always have appreciated the peaceful nature of the sloth. Plus, sloths in the wild are more active than sloths in captivity, so the general characterization of sloths as hardly ever moving is misleading.

Manatee

The manatee is my second favorite animal. I am impressed by its gentle nature. Also, I like how strange it looks.

Panda

Pandas are perhaps not the third, but one of my favorite animals. It amazes me how they can survive on a diet of 99% bamboo.
